Best Bleach Episodes

Genre: Anime, Animation, Action, Fantasy Network: Tv Tokyo

For as long as he can remember, Ichigo Kurosaki has been able to see ghosts. But when he meets Rukia, a Soul Reaper who battles evil spirits known as Hollows, he finds his life is changed forever. Now, with a newfound wealth of spiritual energy, Ichigo discovers his true calling: to protect the living and the dead from evil. Bleach is a Japanese action fantasy anime that was first aired in 2004. See the best episodes of Bleach below.

Best Entourage Episodes

Genre: Drama, Comedy Network: HBO

Film star Vince Chase navigates the vapid terrain of Los Angeles with a close circle of friends and his trusty agent. Entourage is an American comedy drama tv-series that was first aired in 2004 on HBO. See the best episodes of Entourage below.

Best Entourage Episodes (Ranked)

1. The End

Vince takes a surprising step after a first date with Sophia. A therapy session yields mixed results for Ari and Mrs. Ari, and Vince, Drama and Turtle urge Sloan to work things out with Eric.

Director: David Nutter
Writer: Doug Ellin

2. Give a Little Bit

Season 6 - Episode 12October 4, 2009

When Matt Damon and LeBron James send out a charity call, an unwilling Vince takes up the challenge. Ari tries to convince his wife that his deal with Terrance will be lucrative. Drama ponders a switch in careers. Eric makes plans to meet Sloan.

Director: Mark Mylod
Writer: Ally Musika,

3. The Abyss

Writer: Doug Ellin,

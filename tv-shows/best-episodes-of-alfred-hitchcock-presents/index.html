Best Episodes of Alfred Hitchcock Presents

Genre: Drama, Crime Network: CBS

Alfred Hitchcock Presents is an American television anthology series hosted by Alfred Hitchcock. The series featured dramas, thrillers, and mysteries. By the time the show premiered on October 2, 1955, Hitchcock had been directing films for over three decades. Time magazine named Alfred Hitchcock Presents one of The 100 Best TV Shows of All-TIME. A series of literary anthologies with the running title Alfred Hitchcock Presents were issued to capitalize on the success of the television series.

Best Episodes of Beyond Scared Straight

Genre: Reality, Crime Network: A&E

Observing programs for troubled teens in which inmates reveal the realities of prison life in the hope of deterring them from a life of crime. Each hourlong episode follows a handful of at-risk teenagers as they attend a program, which includes an intensive one-day session in a prison. A month later, the teens are asked what, if any, lasting impressions the program had on them. Some change their ways following the experience while others continue down the same path they were on before.See the best Beyond Scared Straight episodes below.

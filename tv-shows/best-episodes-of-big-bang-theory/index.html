Best Episodes of The Big Bang Theory

Genre: Romance, Comedy Network: CBS

The sitcom is centered on five characters living in Pasadena, California: roommates Leonard Hofstadter and Sheldon Cooper; Penny, a waitress and aspiring actress who lives across the hall; and Leonard and Sheldon's equally geeky and socially awkward friends and co-workers, mechanical engineer Howard Wolowitz and astrophysicist Raj Koothrappali. The geekiness and intellect of the four guys is contrasted for comic effect with Penny's social skills and common sense. The Big Bang Theory is an American sitcom tv-series that was first aired in 2007 on CBS. See the best The Big Bang Theory episodes below.

Best Episodes of Law and Order

Genre: Mystery, Crime, Drama Network: NBC

Follows a crime (usually a murder), usually adapted from current headlines, from two separate vantage points, the police investigation and the prosecution in court. Law and Order is an American police and legal drama tv series that was first premiered in 1990. See the best Law and Order episodes below to binge-watch on.

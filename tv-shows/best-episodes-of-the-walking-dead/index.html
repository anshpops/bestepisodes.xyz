Best Episodes of the Walking Dead

Genre: Horror, Action, Adventure Network: AMC

Sheriff's deputy Rick Grimes awakens from a coma to find a post-apocalyptic world dominated by flesh-eating zombies. He sets out to find his family and encounters many other survivors along the way. The Walking Dead is an American horror, action & adventure tv-series that was first aired in 2010. See The Walking Dead best episodes below.

Best Episodes of Wizards of Waverly Place

Genre: Comedy, Family Network: Disney

Alex, Justin and Max Russo are not your ordinary kids - they're wizards in training! While their parents run the Waverly Sub Station, the siblings struggle to balance their ordinary lives while learning to master their extraordinary powers. Wizards of Waverly Place is an American family teen comedy tv-series that was first aired in 2007. See Wizards of Waverly Place best episodes below.

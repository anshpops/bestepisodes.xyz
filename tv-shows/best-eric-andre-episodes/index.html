A comedic talk show from an alternate reality featuring unstable hosts, a variety of celebrities—both real and fake—and unusual studio action. The Eric Andre Show is an American comedy tv-show that first premiered in 2012 and is a parody of talk-shows. See the best episodes of Eric Andre Show below.

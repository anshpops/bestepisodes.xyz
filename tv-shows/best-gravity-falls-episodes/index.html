Best Gravity Falls Episodes

Genre: Animation, Comedy, Family Network: Disney

Twin brother and sister Dipper and Mabel Pines are in for an unexpected adventure when they spend the summer helping their great uncle Stan run a tourist trap in the mysterious town of Gravity Falls, Oregon. Gravity Falls is an animated American comedy tv-series that was first aired in 2012. See the best episodes of Gravity Falls below.

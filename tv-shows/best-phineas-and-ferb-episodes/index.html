Best Phineas and Ferb Episodes

Genre: Comedy, Animation, Family Network: Disney

Each day, two kindhearted suburban stepbrothers on summer vacation embark on some grand new project, which annoys their controlling sister, Candace, who tries to bust them. Meanwhile, their pet platypus plots against evil Dr. Doofenshmirtz. Phineas and Ferb is an American animated comedy tv-series that was first aired in 2007. See the best episodes of Phineas and Ferb below.

Best Phineas and Ferb Episodes (Ranked)

1. Night of the Living Pharmacists

Season 4 - Episode 44October 4, 2014

Doofenshmirtz's latest "inator" turns Danville citizens into contagious zombies. Agent P. and the gang try to save the town and avoid becoming zombies themselves.

Director: Robert F. Hughes,

2. Phineas and Ferb: Star Wars

Season 4 - Episode 41July 26, 2014

In a parallel universe far, far away, Phineas and Ferb are tasked with returning the Death Star plans to the Rebel Alliance before Storm trooper Candace catches up to them. Meanwhile, complications arise when Perry is trapped in carbonite.

Director: Robert F. Hughes,

3. Phineas and Ferb Musical Cliptastic Countdown

Season 2 - Episode 26October 16, 2009

Fortunately, Perry the Platypus is there to save the day.

Director: Robert F. Hughes

4. Act Your Age

Season 4 - Episode 47February 9, 2015

It's been 10 years after the events of the present, and Phineas must decide what university to go to. After discovering that Isabella had a crush on him for years, Phineas must reach her before she leaves for college and express his true feelings for her. Meanwhile, Doofenshmirtz has a mid-life crisis.

Director: Robert F. Hughes

5. Summer Belongs To You

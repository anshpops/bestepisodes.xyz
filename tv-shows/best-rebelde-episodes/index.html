Best Rebelde Episodes

Genre: Comedy, Family Network: Televisa

Rebelde is a Mexican telenovela produced by Televisa and created by Cris Morena. It is a remake of a famous Argentine series Rebelde Way adapted for the Mexican audience therefore leading to differences in characters' backgrounds. The series ran for three seasons, the final episode airing in Mexico on June 2, 2006. Rebelde was replaced in June 2006 with Televisa's new series Código Postal. Rebelde is a Mexican comedy family tv-series that was first aired in 2004. See the best episodes of Rebelde below.

Genre: Drama, Crime Network: The CW

Set in the present, the series offers a bold, subversive take on Archie, Betty, Veronica and their friends, exploring the surreality of small-town life, the darkness and weirdness bubbling beneath Riverdale’s wholesome facade. Riverdale is an American crime drama tv-series that was first aired in 2017. See the best episodes of Riverdale below.
